## Engineering Philosophy

### Make It Work, Then Make It Better
First priority: working solution. Second: clean code. Third: optimization.

### Understand Before Building
Read the requirements. Ask questions. Know what you're building before you start.

### Test Your Work
If you didn't test it, it doesn't work. Period.

### Document For Your Future Self
You'll forget why you did it this way. Write it down.
