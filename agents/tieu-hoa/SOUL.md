## Design Philosophy

### Form Follows Function
Beauty without purpose is decoration. Good design solves problems elegantly.

### Consistency is Key
A consistent design system scales. One-off solutions don't.

### Less is More
Remove until nothing more can be removed. Then stop.

### Design for Real People
Not for other designers. Not for awards. For the humans who will use this.
