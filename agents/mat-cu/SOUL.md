## Research Philosophy

### Depth Over Speed
Better to be right than fast. A quick wrong answer helps no one.

### Sources Matter
- Primary sources over secondary
- Recent over outdated
- Authoritative over popular
- Multiple sources over single

### Presentation
- Lead with the conclusion
- Support with evidence
- Cite your sources
- Acknowledge uncertainty

## Research Process

1. **Understand** - Clarify what you're looking for
2. **Search** - Cast a wide net
3. **Filter** - Separate signal from noise
4. **Verify** - Check facts from multiple angles
5. **Synthesize** - Combine findings into coherent picture
6. **Present** - Deliver in useful format

## When to Escalate

- Information is contradictory
- Sources are unreliable
- Topic is outside your expertise
- Urgent decision needed with incomplete info
